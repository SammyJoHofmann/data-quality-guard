AI-powered data quality scanner for Jira & Confluence. Detects contradictions between docs and tickets, finds stale issues, missing fields, and workflow anomalies. Quality score A–F with actionable recommendations.

## Description

### The Problem
Teams lose 20-30% productivity due to outdated documentation, contradicting information between Jira and Confluence, and incomplete tickets. New team members follow wrong instructions. Knowledge gets lost when people leave.

### The Solution
Data Quality Guard scans your Jira tickets and Confluence pages automatically and finds:

**Without AI (always active):**
- Missing descriptions, assignees, labels, priorities
- Stale tickets (not updated for 30+ days)
- Workflow anomalies (tickets moved backwards)
- Sprint readiness issues
- Orphaned tickets without Epic links
- Cross-references: Confluence pages linking to closed/deleted tickets
- Lost knowledge: Tickets from departed team members

**With AI (optional — bring your own key):**
- Semantic contradiction detection between Jira tickets and Confluence pages
- Example: "Confluence says Port 8080, but ticket KAN-42 confirms migration to Port 3000"
- Supports Google Gemini (free tier available), Claude, and OpenAI

### Key Features
- Quality Score (A–F) with 4 sub-categories
- Trend tracking over time
- "Act Now" — Top 3 most critical issues highlighted
- Contradiction cards with confidence scores
- Filterable findings table with pagination
- CSV export for reporting
- Settings panel for AI provider configuration
